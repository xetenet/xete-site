A simple guide

This is xete. Here's what it does, and exactly where to find each part.

Written in plain language on purpose — for app store reviewers, and for anyone else who just wants the short version.

What is xete?

xete is an app for a phone. It does three things: it lets you send messages that only the other person can read, it lets you trade coins and collectibles directly with other people, and it gives you a locked, private place to keep files. Nobody but you can read your messages or touch your money — not even us, the people who made the app.

What phone do I need?

xete needs a Solana Saga or Solana Seeker phone. Those phones have a special locked box built into them called Seed Vault. Your secret key — the thing that proves the wallet is yours — lives inside that box and never comes out. That's what keeps it safe.

If you're testing this app and don't have one of those phones on hand: Solana Mobile publishes a free tool called the Seed Vault Simulator. It makes a normal Android phone or emulator act like it has a Seed Vault, just for testing. You can find it at docs.solanamobile.com/developers/seed-vault.

Opening the app for the first time

  1. Open xete.
  2. Tap the button that says "Unlock."
  3. Your phone will ask you, twice, if it's okay to make a new secret key. Say yes both times. (One key is your identity. The other is for your messages.)
  4. You'll land on your Home screen. It looks like a single card — like an ID card.

The card is the whole app — here's what each move does

There's no row of buttons at the bottom of the screen. Instead, you move the card itself:

  • Swipe the card down → opens your Wallet (your money and the things you own).
  • Swipe the card left → opens your Messages.
  • Swipe the card right → shows a QR code, so someone else can scan it to add you.
  • Swipe the card up → it flips around to 3 buttons: Contacts, Explorer, and Settings.
  • Press and hold the small fingerprint icon on the card → opens your Vault (a locked box for files).

Things you can try that don't cost anything Free

  • Open Messages and say hi to %echo. %echo is a friendly robot that lives inside the app. It writes back right away and can show you around the rest of the features.
  • Open Explorer to look at trades and NFT collections other people have posted.
  • Open your Vault and lock a file inside it.

Things that use a little real money Small cost

Solana (the network xete runs on) is a public record book that anyone can check but nobody can erase. Writing something into that book costs a tiny fee, paid in real SOL (usually well under a dollar). Two things in xete do this:

  • Claiming a name for yourself (like %yourname) — a short name that points to your wallet, so people don't have to remember a long address.
  • Trading or swapping — buying, selling, or exchanging real coins and collectibles is a real transaction, the same as it would be on any exchange.

Where to find trading

From your Wallet, look for Trade. It has two tabs:

  • Instant — swap one coin for another right away, at the current market price.
  • OTC (short for "over the counter") — buy or sell an NFT or a token directly with another person. No company sits in the middle holding anyone's money.
