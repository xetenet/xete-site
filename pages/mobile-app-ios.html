xete for iPhone In progress

xete is coming to iPhone. It isn't out yet.

Everything xete does on Solana Saga and Seeker — private messages, direct trading, a locked vault, one identity only you control — is being brought to iPhone next.

What's already built

The core of xete — the part that makes your keys, encrypts your messages, and signs your transactions — is written once and shared across every version of the app, Android and iOS alike. That shared core, and the extra safety check that stops an iPhone from ever signing away your whole account by mistake, are both already built and tested.

What's not there yet

The iPhone app itself — the screens you'd actually tap through — hasn't been finished or released. There's no download link yet. This page will turn into the real thing once there is.

Want the Android version instead?

xete is live today on Solana Saga and Solana Seeker. See what it does and how it works.
