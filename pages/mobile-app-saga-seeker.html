The xete mobile app

One card. Your identity, your messages, your trades, your vault.

xete is a phone app for Solana Saga and Solana Seeker. It turns your phone into a private inbox, a place to trade directly with other people, and a locked box for your files — all under one identity that only you hold the key to.

What the app does

  • Messages — send notes that only the other person can read. Nobody in between, including us, can open them.
  • Names — claim a short name, like %yourname, so people can reach you without typing a long wallet address.
  • Trade — swap coins instantly, or trade an NFT/token directly with another person, no company holding anyone's money in between.
  • Vault — a locked, private place on your phone to keep files.

Built for Saga & Seeker

Solana Saga and Solana Seeker have a special locked chip built in called Seed Vault. Your secret key — the thing that proves your wallet and your identity are really yours — is generated inside that chip and never leaves it. That's what lets xete promise nobody, not even the people who built xete, can read your messages, move your money, or pretend to be you.

Developers and testers working without one of these phones on hand can use Solana Mobile's own Seed Vault Simulator to try the app on a regular Android device or emulator: docs.solanamobile.com/developers/seed-vault.

How it feels to use

There's no row of buttons at the bottom of the screen. Your whole identity lives on one card, and you move the card to get where you're going:

  • Swipe the card down for your Wallet.
  • Swipe the card left for your Messages.
  • Swipe the card right to show your QR code, so someone can scan it to add you.
  • Swipe the card up and it flips around to Contacts, Explorer, and Settings.
  • Press and hold the fingerprint icon on the card for your Vault.

Say hello to %echo

The first thing most people do is open Messages and say hi to %echo — a friendly guide that lives right inside the app. It answers immediately and shows you around the rest of xete. Free, no wallet balance required. Free

What things cost

Messaging, browsing, and talking to %echo cost nothing. Two things use a small amount of real SOL, because they write permanently onto Solana's public ledger: Small cost

  • Claiming a %name for yourself (usually well under a dollar).
  • Trading or swapping — a real transaction, the same as on any exchange.

Trade, two ways

Open your Wallet and look for Trade:

  • Instant — swap one coin for another right away, at the current market price.
  • OTC (over the counter) — buy or sell an NFT or token directly with another person, contract-enforced, with no company holding the assets in between.
